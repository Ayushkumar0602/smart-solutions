How It Works

Simple steps to boost your productivity with the Pomodoro technique

Set Your Timer

Choose your work duration (typically 25 minutes) and break duration (typically 5 minutes). Customize these to fit your workflow.

Focus on Work

Work on your task with complete focus until the timer rings. The app helps minimize distractions and keeps you in the zone.

Take a Short Break

When the timer rings, take a short 5-minute break. Stand up, stretch, or do something enjoyable to refresh your mind.

After Four Pomodoros, Take a Longer Break

After four work sessions, take a longer break (15-30 minutes) to recharge before starting the next set of Pomodoros.
